About
Fort Blocks

Welcome to FortBlocks, the world's safest Bitcoin cold storage facility.

You want to invest in the future potential of Blockchain technologies and are looking to invest in the gold standard, which is Bitcoin. The single biggest challenge is doing it safely, securely and with peace of mind.

Buying and storing digital currency is a complex process with multiple potential obstacles and pitfalls. Navigating these independently is possible when dealing with relatively small sums but once you're investing in the hundreds of thousands and millions, you need to make sure you're covered at the highest level, in the same way you are when you invest in fiat.

The most important factor in protecting your investment is storage security. Beyond any price fluctuation or external variables, you want peace of mind that your investment is protected and secure from personal error; loss or a compromise of hardware or software; malware or hackers.

FortBlocks has set a new standard for the secure storage of cryptocurrencies, specialising in long term Bitcoin investments for corporations and individuals. FortBlocks removes the complexity and concerns associated with digital currency investment; managing the process end-to-end to ensure the complete security of your investments.
